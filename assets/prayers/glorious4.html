4th glorious mystery - The Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary into Heaven

And he came to her and said, “Hail, full of grace, the Lord is with you! (Luke 1:28)

Being full of grace and free from bodily corruption, Mary is taken bodily into heaven where she prays for Her sons and daughters, those sons and daughters Jesus left Her when He was on the cross. Mary, mediatrix of all graces, pray for us, You know our fears, take us into Your Heart, the Heart of a Mother. Help us now and in the time of our death to be with You in Heaven.

In Latin: Quartum mystérium gloriósum - Assumptiónem Beátæ Maríæ Vírginis in cáelum
