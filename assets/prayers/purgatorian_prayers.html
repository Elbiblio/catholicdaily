Prayer for the Souls in Purgatory
O God, Creator and Redeemer of all, to Whom it belongs always to have mercy and to spare, we humbly commend to You the souls of the faithful departed, that, through the infinite merits of Your Divine Son, the intercession of the Blessed Virgin Mary, Saint Michael the Archangel and all Your saints, You release them from Purgatory and permit them to be received by Your holy angels and conducted into the eternal bliss of Paradise.

May the souls of all the faithful departed through the mercy of God rest in peace. Amen.

Prayer of Saint Gertrude for Poor Souls in Purgatory
Eternal Father, I offer You the Most Precious Blood of the Your Divine Son, Jesus, in union with the Masses said throughout the world today, for all the holy souls in purgatory, for sinners everywhere, for sinners in the universal Church, those in my own home and within my family.  Amen.
