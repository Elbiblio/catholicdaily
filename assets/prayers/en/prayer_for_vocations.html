Prayer for Vocations

Loving and generous God, it is Thou who callest us by name and askest us to follow Thee. Help us to grow in the love and service of our Church as we experience it today.

Give us the energy and courage of Thy Spirit to shape its future. Grant us faith-filled leaders who will embrace Christ's mission of love and justice.

Bless the Church by raising up dedicated and generous leaders from our families and friends who will serve as sisters, priests, brothers, deacons, and lay ministers.

Inspire us to know Thee better and open our hearts to hear Thy call. We ask this through our Lord Jesus Christ and through the intercession of His Blessed Mother. Amen.

Lord of the harvest, send laborers into Thy harvest.
