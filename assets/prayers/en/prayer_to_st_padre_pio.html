Prayer to St. Padre Pio

Beloved St. Padre Pio, thou who bore the sacred wounds of our Lord Jesus Christ on thy body, and who suffered greatly out of love for God and for the salvation of souls, I ask thee to intercede for me before the throne of God.

Through thy powerful intercession, I ask for the grace of (state your intention), and above all, the grace to live and die in the state of sanctifying grace.

Help me to always trust in God's mercy, to bear my crosses with patience, and to grow in love for our Lord Jesus Christ and His Blessed Mother.

Pray for me, dear St. Padre Pio, that I may be faithful to my duties and grow in holiness each day. May I, like thee, love the Holy Mass, the Rosary, and the Sacrament of Confession with all my heart.

O God, Thou gavest St. Padre Pio the privilege of participating in a mysterious way in the Passion of Thy Son. Grant me through his intercession the grace of (mention your request) which I ardently desire; and above all grant me the grace of living in Thy love and dying in Thy grace. Amen.

St. Padre Pio, pray for us.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.
