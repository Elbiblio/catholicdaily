Prayer After Confession

Almighty and merciful God, I thank Thee for the grace of this sacrament. I thank Thee for Thy forgiveness and Thy love. Help me to persevere in Thy grace and to avoid the occasions of sin.

Strengthen my will to do what is right and give me the grace to fulfill the penance I have received. May this confession bring me closer to Thee and help me to live a holier life.

O Mary, my Mother, pray for me that I may remain faithful to the graces I have received today. St. Joseph, guardian of the Holy Family, protect me from all temptation. My Guardian Angel, guide me on the path of holiness.

Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit, as it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.
