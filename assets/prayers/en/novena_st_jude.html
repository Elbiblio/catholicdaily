Novena to St. Jude Thaddeus
Patron Saint of Hopeless and Desperate Cases. Pray for nine consecutive days.

Daily Prayer

O most holy Apostle, St. Jude Thaddeus, faithful servant and friend of Jesus, the name of the traitor who delivered thy beloved Master into the hands of His enemies hath caused thee to be forgotten by many; but the Church honors and invokes thee universally as the patron of hopeless cases, of things almost despaired of.

Pray for me, I am so helpless and alone. Make use, I implore thee, of that particular privilege given to thee, to bring visible and speedy help where help is almost despaired of. Come to my assistance in this great need, that I may receive the consolation and help of heaven in all my necessities, tribulations, and sufferings, particularly (state your request), and that I may praise God with thee and all the elect forever.

I promise, O blessed St. Jude, to be ever mindful of this great favor, to always honor thee as my special and powerful patron, and to gratefully encourage devotion to thee. Amen.

St. Jude, pray for us and for all who invoke thy aid.

Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.
