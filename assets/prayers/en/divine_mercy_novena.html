Novena to the Divine Mercy
As taught by Our Lord Jesus Christ to St. Faustina. Begin on Good Friday, conclude on Divine Mercy Sunday.

Opening Prayer (each day)
You expired, Jesus, but the source of life gushed forth for souls, and the ocean of mercy opened up for the whole world. O Fount of Life, unfathomable Divine Mercy, envelop the whole world and empty Yourself out upon us.
(Repeat three times:)
O Blood and Water, which gushed forth from the Heart of Jesus as a fount of mercy for us, I trust in You!

Day 1 - All Mankind, Especially Sinners
Today bring to Me all mankind, especially all sinners.
Most Merciful Jesus, whose very nature it is to have compassion on us and to forgive us, do not look upon our sins but upon our trust which we place in Thy infinite goodness. Receive us all into the abode of Thy Most Compassionate Heart, and never let us escape from It. We beg this of Thee by Thy love which unites Thee to the Father and the Holy Spirit.
Eternal Father, turn Thy merciful gaze upon all mankind and especially upon poor sinners, all enfolded in the Most Compassionate Heart of Jesus. For the sake of His sorrowful Passion, show us Thy mercy, that we may praise the omnipotence of Thy mercy forever and ever. Amen.

Day 2 - Priests and Religious
Today bring to Me the souls of priests and religious.
Most Merciful Jesus, from whom comes all that is good, increase Thy grace in men and women consecrated to Thy service, that they may perform worthy works of mercy; and that all who see them may glorify the Father of Mercy who is in heaven.
Eternal Father, turn Thy merciful gaze upon the company of chosen ones in Thy vineyard. For the sake of His sorrowful Passion, grant them the power of Thy blessing, and for the love of the Heart of Thy Son in which they are enfolded, impart to them Thy power and light, that they may be able to guide others in the way of salvation. Amen.

Day 3 - All Devout and Faithful Souls
Today bring to Me all devout and faithful souls.
Most Merciful Jesus, from the treasury of Thy graces, Thou impartest Thy gifts in great abundance to each and all. Receive us into the abode of Thy Most Compassionate Heart and never let us escape from It. We beg this grace of Thee by that most wondrous love for the heavenly Father with which Thy Heart burns so fiercely.
Eternal Father, turn Thy merciful gaze upon faithful souls, as upon the inheritance of Thy Son. For the sake of His sorrowful Passion, grant them Thy blessing and surround them with Thy constant protection. Amen.

Day 4 - Those Who Do Not Believe and Those Who Do Not Yet Know Jesus
Today bring to Me those who do not believe in God and those who do not yet know Me.
Most Compassionate Jesus, Thou art the Light of the whole world. Receive into the abode of Thy Most Compassionate Heart the souls of those who do not believe in God and of those who as yet do not know Thee. Let the rays of Thy grace enlighten them that they, too, together with us, may extol Thy wonderful mercy.
Eternal Father, turn Thy merciful gaze upon the souls of those who do not believe in Thee, and of those who as yet do not know Thee, but who are enclosed in the Most Compassionate Heart of Jesus. Draw them to the light of the Gospel. Grant them the grace to recognize the true beauty of Thy love. Amen.

Day 5 - Souls of Separated Brethren
Today bring to Me the souls of those who have separated themselves from My Church.
Most Merciful Jesus, Goodness Itself, Thou dost not refuse light to those who seek it of Thee. Receive into the abode of Thy Most Compassionate Heart the souls of those who have separated themselves from Thy Church. Draw them by Thy light into the unity of the Church, and do not let them escape from the abode of Thy Most Compassionate Heart.
Eternal Father, turn Thy merciful gaze upon the souls of those who have separated themselves from Thy Son's Church. Grant them the grace of unity with the Church. Amen.

Day 6 - The Meek and Humble
Today bring to Me the meek and humble souls and the souls of little children.
Most Merciful Jesus, Thou Thyself hast said, "Learn from Me for I am meek and humble of heart." Receive into the abode of Thy Most Compassionate Heart all meek and humble souls and the souls of little children. These souls send all heaven into ecstasy, and they are the heavenly Father's favorites.
Eternal Father, turn Thy merciful gaze upon meek souls, upon humble souls, and upon little children who are enfolded in the abode which is the Most Compassionate Heart of Jesus. Grant that they may reach the glory of heaven. Amen.

Day 7 - Those Who Especially Venerate Divine Mercy
Today bring to Me the souls who especially venerate and glorify My mercy.
Most Merciful Jesus, whose Heart is Love Itself, receive into the abode of Thy Most Compassionate Heart the souls of those who especially extol and venerate the greatness of Thy mercy. Give them the grace to grow in mercy and to be witnesses of Thy love in the world.
Eternal Father, turn Thy merciful gaze upon the souls who glorify and venerate Thy greatest attribute, that of Thy fathomless mercy, and who are enclosed in the Most Compassionate Heart of Jesus. Amen.

Day 8 - The Souls in Purgatory
Today bring to Me the souls who are detained in Purgatory.
Most Merciful Jesus, Thou Thyself hast said that Thou desirest mercy; so I bring to the abode of Thy Most Compassionate Heart the souls in Purgatory, souls who are very dear to Thee, and yet who must make retribution to Thy justice. May the streams of Blood and Water which gushed forth from Thy Heart put out the flames of Purgatory.
Eternal Father, turn Thy merciful gaze upon the souls suffering in Purgatory, who are enfolded in the Most Compassionate Heart of Jesus. Grant them relief from their suffering. Amen.

Day 9 - Souls Who Have Become Lukewarm
Today bring to Me the souls who have become lukewarm.
Most Compassionate Jesus, Thou art Compassion Itself. I bring lukewarm souls into the abode of Thy Most Compassionate Heart. In this fire of Thy pure love, let these tepid souls, who like corpses filled Thee with such deep loathing, be once again set aflame.
Eternal Father, turn Thy merciful gaze upon lukewarm souls who are nonetheless enfolded in the Most Compassionate Heart of Jesus. Father of Mercy, let the fire of Thy love rekindle the dying embers of grace within them. Amen.

After each day's prayer, pray the Chaplet of Divine Mercy.
