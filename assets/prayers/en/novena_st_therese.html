Novena to St. Therese of Lisieux (The Little Flower)
Pray for nine consecutive days.

Daily Prayer

O Little Therese of the Child Jesus, who during thy short life on earth became a mirror of angelic purity, of love strong as death, and of wholehearted abandonment to God, now that thou rejoicest in the reward of thy virtues, cast a glance of pity on me as I leave all things in thy hands.

Make my troubles thine own. Speak a word for me to our Lady Immaculate, whose flower of special love thou wert, to that Queen of Heaven who smiled on thee at the dawn of life.

Beg her as the Queen of all hearts to obtain for me by her powerful intercession the grace I yearn for so ardently at this moment (state your request), and that she join with it a blessing that may strengthen me during life, defend me at the hour of death, and lead me straight to a happy eternity. Amen.

O Little Therese, remember thy promise to spend thy heaven doing good upon the earth. Send down a shower of roses upon me. Obtain for me the graces I need, and let me feel thy closeness to God.

Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

St. Therese of the Child Jesus, pray for us.
