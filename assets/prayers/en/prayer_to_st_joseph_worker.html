Prayer to St. Joseph the Worker

O Glorious St. Joseph, model of all who are devoted to labor, obtain for me the grace to work in the spirit of penance in atonement for my many sins; to work conscientiously by placing love of duty above my inclinations; to gratefully and joyously deem it an honor to employ and to develop by labor the gifts I have received from God; to work methodically, peacefully, and in moderation and patience, without ever shrinking from it through weariness or difficulty.

Help me to work, above all, with purity of intention and unselfishness, having unceasingly before my eyes the hour of my accounting and the reward which is promised.

Glorious St. Joseph, patron of workers, intercede for all those who labor, that they may find worthy employment and just wages. Protect those who are unemployed, underemployed, or exploited. May all workers be treated with dignity and respect.

St. Joseph the Worker, pray for us. Amen.
