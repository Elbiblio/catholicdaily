Novena to the Sacred Heart of Jesus
Pray for nine consecutive days, especially before the Feast of the Sacred Heart.

Opening Prayer (each day)
O most holy Heart of Jesus, fountain of every blessing, I adore Thee, I love Thee, and with a lively sorrow for my sins, I offer Thee this poor heart of mine. Make me humble, patient, pure, and wholly obedient to Thy will. Grant, good Jesus, that I may live in Thee and for Thee. Protect me in the midst of danger; comfort me in my afflictions. Give me health of body, assistance in my temporal needs, Thy blessing on all that I do, and the grace of a holy death. Amen.

Day 1 - For Fervor in the Service of God
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, I come to Thee with heartfelt sorrow for my sins. With a firm purpose of amendment, I consecrate myself entirely to Thee. Grant me, I beseech Thee, the grace to serve Thee with fervor and fidelity all the days of my life.
Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

Day 2 - For Purity of Intention
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, purify my intentions that I may do all things for Thy greater glory. Teach me to seek Thee in all my actions, and to desire nothing but Thy holy will in every moment of my life.
Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

Day 3 - For Charity Toward Others
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, inflame my heart with that charity which burned in Thine own Heart. Teach me to love my neighbor as myself, to forgive those who have offended me, and to bear with patience the faults of others.
Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

Day 4 - For Patience in Suffering
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, grant me the grace to bear my crosses and sufferings with patience and resignation to Thy holy will. Unite my sufferings with Thine for the salvation of souls.
Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

Day 5 - For Humility
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, meek and humble of heart, make my heart like unto Thine. Free me from all pride and vanity, and grant me the grace of true humility.
Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

Day 6 - For Trust in God
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, in whom I find all my refuge and my strength, I trust in Thee with all my heart. Thou who hast promised that whatever we ask the Father in Thy name shall be granted, hear my prayer and grant my petition.
Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

Day 7 - For the Conversion of Sinners
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, have mercy on sinners. Touch the hearts of those who have wandered from Thee, and bring them back to Thy love. May Thy mercy triumph over all sin and evil.
Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

Day 8 - For the Souls in Purgatory
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, have mercy on the souls in Purgatory. May Thy Precious Blood relieve their sufferings, and grant them eternal rest in Thy presence. Remember especially those who have no one to pray for them.
Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

Day 9 - For the Grace of Final Perseverance
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, grant me the grace of final perseverance. May I remain faithful to Thee until the end of my life, and die in Thy love and in the state of grace. Grant me the joy of beholding Thee face to face in heaven.
Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be.

Closing Prayer (each day):
O Sacred Heart of Jesus, I believe in Thy love for me. I love Thee with all my heart. I am sorry for all my sins. I will try never to offend Thee again. I will do all that Thou askest of me. I accept all the suffering Thou sendest me. Grant me the grace to love Thee more and more. Sacred Heart of Jesus, I place all my trust in Thee. Amen.
