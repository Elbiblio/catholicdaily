Novena to the Holy Spirit
The original novena, traditionally prayed between Ascension Thursday and Pentecost Sunday.

Daily Prayer for Each of the Nine Days

Come, Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of Thy faithful and enkindle in them the fire of Thy love.
V. Send forth Thy Spirit, and they shall be created.
R. And Thou shalt renew the face of the earth.

Let us pray.
O God, Who by the light of the Holy Spirit, didst instruct the hearts of the faithful, grant that by the same Holy Spirit we may be truly wise and ever enjoy His consolations. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.

Day 1 - The Holy Spirit, Spirit of Truth
O Holy Spirit, Spirit of Truth, come into our hearts; shed the brightness of Thy light on all nations, that they may be one in faith and pleasing to Thee. Come, O Spirit of Wisdom, and teach us to value the things of heaven. Come, and reveal to our souls the treasures of grace.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be. (seven times)

Day 2 - The Holy Spirit, Spirit of Wisdom
O Holy Spirit, grant us the gift of Wisdom, that we may despise the perishable things of this world and aspire only after the things that are eternal. May we discern in all things the hand of God and perceive the beauty of His love.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be. (seven times)

Day 3 - The Holy Spirit, Spirit of Understanding
O Holy Spirit, grant us the gift of Understanding, that we may comprehend the words and teachings of the Lord, and penetrate the inner meaning of the revealed truths. Illuminate our minds with Thy heavenly light.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be. (seven times)

Day 4 - The Holy Spirit, Spirit of Counsel
O Holy Spirit, grant us the gift of Counsel, that in all things we may know Thy holy will and take the right course of action. Guide us through the difficulties and decisions of daily life, and grant us prudence in all our undertakings.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be. (seven times)

Day 5 - The Holy Spirit, Spirit of Fortitude
O Holy Spirit, grant us the gift of Fortitude, that we may bear our cross with Thee, and that we may overcome with courage all the obstacles that oppose our salvation. Give us strength to resist temptation and to endure suffering with patience.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be. (seven times)

Day 6 - The Holy Spirit, Spirit of Knowledge
O Holy Spirit, grant us the gift of Knowledge, that we may know God and know ourselves, and learn the true value of all created things. Teach us to use the goods of this world only for Thy glory and for the salvation of souls.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be. (seven times)

Day 7 - The Holy Spirit, Spirit of Piety
O Holy Spirit, grant us the gift of Piety, that we may serve Thee with a loving heart and find consolation in prayer and worship. Fill our hearts with tender devotion to God our Father and a childlike trust in His providence.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be. (seven times)

Day 8 - The Holy Spirit, Spirit of Holy Fear
O Holy Spirit, grant us the gift of Holy Fear, that we may be filled with a loving reverence towards God, and may dread in all things to displease Him. Keep us from sin by the awe of Thy majesty and the greatness of Thy love.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be. (seven times)

Day 9 - The Fruits of the Holy Spirit
O Holy Spirit, by Thy powerful intercession, grant us all the gifts and fruits of the Spirit: charity, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control. Transform our hearts and make us living temples of Thy grace.
Our Father. Hail Mary. Glory Be. (seven times)

Concluding Prayer (each day):
Come, O Holy Spirit, replenish the hearts of Thy faithful, and enkindle in them the fire of Thy love. Send forth Thy Spirit, and they shall be created, and Thou shalt renew the face of the earth. Amen.
