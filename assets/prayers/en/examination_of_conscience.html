Examination of Conscience Based on the Ten Commandments

Begin with a prayer to the Holy Spirit:
Come, Holy Spirit, enlighten my mind that I may clearly know my sins. Move my heart that I may be sincerely sorry for them, honestly confess them, and firmly resolve to amend my life. Amen.

1. I am the Lord your God; you shall not have strange gods before me.
- Have I doubted or denied the existence of God?
- Have I failed to pray daily?
- Have I placed excessive trust in money, career, or possessions?
- Have I been involved in superstition, the occult, or New Age practices?
- Have I received Holy Communion in a state of mortal sin?
- Have I neglected to mention serious sins in past confessions?

2. You shall not take the name of the Lord your God in vain.
- Have I used the name of God, Jesus, Mary, or the saints irreverently?
- Have I cursed or sworn falsely?
- Have I spoken disrespectfully about sacred things?
- Have I broken a vow or promise made to God?

3. Remember to keep holy the Lord's Day.
- Have I missed Mass on Sundays or Holy Days of Obligation through my own fault?
- Have I been late for Mass or left early without good reason?
- Have I been inattentive or irreverent during Mass?
- Have I done unnecessary work on Sunday?

4. Honor your father and your mother.
- Have I disobeyed or disrespected my parents or legitimate authorities?
- Have I neglected to care for elderly parents or family members?
- Have I caused my family pain through selfishness or neglect?
- Have I failed to give good example to my children?

5. You shall not kill.
- Have I harmed anyone physically or wished them harm?
- Have I been angry, resentful, or refused to forgive others?
- Have I abused alcohol or drugs?
- Have I given scandal by my words or actions?
- Have I neglected to take proper care of my health?

6. You shall not commit adultery.
- Have I been unfaithful in marriage in thought, word, or deed?
- Have I engaged in impure thoughts or actions?
- Have I used impure or suggestive language?
- Have I failed to practice modesty in dress or behavior?

7. You shall not steal.
- Have I stolen or damaged the property of others?
- Have I been dishonest in business or work?
- Have I failed to pay just debts or wages?
- Have I been wasteful of natural resources?
- Have I neglected to give to the poor and the Church according to my means?

8. You shall not bear false witness against your neighbor.
- Have I lied or deceived others?
- Have I spoken ill of others (detraction) or spread false information (calumny)?
- Have I gossiped or revealed secrets entrusted to me?
- Have I judged others rashly or uncharitably?

9. You shall not covet your neighbor's wife.
- Have I deliberately entertained impure thoughts or desires?
- Have I failed to guard my senses and imagination?
- Have I exposed myself to occasions of impurity?

10. You shall not covet your neighbor's goods.
- Have I been envious of the possessions or talents of others?
- Have I been greedy or materialistic?
- Have I been ungrateful for the blessings God has given me?

Conclude with an Act of Contrition:
O my God, I am heartily sorry for having offended Thee, and I detest all my sins because of Thy just punishments, but most of all because they offend Thee, my God, Who art all good and deserving of all my love. I firmly resolve, with the help of Thy grace, to sin no more and to avoid the near occasions of sin. Amen.
