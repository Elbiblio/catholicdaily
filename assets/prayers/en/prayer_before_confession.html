Prayer Before Confession

O Most Merciful God, I come before Thee as a sinner in need of Thy forgiveness. Grant me the grace to make a good confession. Help me to examine my conscience carefully, to be truly sorry for my sins, and to confess them honestly.

Give me the courage to acknowledge my faults and the humility to accept the penance I receive. Through the ministry of Thy priest, grant me Thy pardon and peace.

Holy Spirit, enlighten my mind to know my sins and move my heart to sincere repentance. Blessed Mother Mary, pray for me that I may receive this sacrament worthily and with a contrite heart.

I trust in Thy infinite mercy, O Lord. Amen.
