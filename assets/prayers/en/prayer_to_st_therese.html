Prayer to St. Therese of Lisieux (The Little Flower)

O Little Therese of the Child Jesus, please pick for me a rose from the heavenly gardens and send it to me as a message of love.

O Little Flower of Jesus, ask God to grant the favors I now place with confidence in thy hands (mention your request).

St. Therese, help me to always believe, as thou didst, in God's great love for me, so that I might imitate thy Little Way each day.

O glorious St. Therese, whom Almighty God has raised up to aid and inspire the human family, I implore thine intercession. Thou knowest my struggles and all the bitterness of my heart. Thou knowest how surrounded I am with thorns. Deign to send me a rose of grace. Remind me always that above the dark clouds, the Son of Justice shines forever.

St. Therese of the Child Jesus, pray for us. Amen.
