Prayer to My Guardian Angel

Angel of God, my guardian dear,
to whom God's love commits me here,
ever this day be at my side,
to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.

Extended Prayer to the Guardian Angel

O Holy Angel, whom God, by the effect of His goodness and His tender regard for my welfare, has charged with the care of my conduct, and who assists me in all my wants and comforts me in all my afflictions, who supports me when I am discouraged and continually obtains for me new favors:

I return thee profound thanks, and I earnestly beseech thee, O most amiable protector, to continue thy charitable care and defense of me against the wicked attacks of all my enemies.

Guard me when I sleep, and inspire me when I am awake. Protect me in every moment of my life, and especially at the hour of my death. Keep me always in the way of holiness, and console me by thy amiable presence and loving protection.

O Angel of God, my blessed guardian, to whom I have been entrusted since the first moment of my life, today and every day, give me your light and your protection. Amen.

Holy Guardian Angel, pray for me.
