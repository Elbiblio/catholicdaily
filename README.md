## Features

- **Liturgical Calendar** — Full Catholic calendar with feast days, solemnities, memorials, and liturgical seasons through 2038.
- **Daily Mass Readings** — First Reading, Responsorial Psalm, Gospel Acclamation, and Gospel for every day, with colour-coded labels. Covers Sunday cycles A, B & C, and both weekday years.
- **RSV-CE Bible Offline** — Complete Revised Standard Version Catholic Edition with search, bookmarks, and text-to-speech — no internet needed.
- **100+ Traditional Prayers** — The Holy Rosary (all four Mysteries), Divine Mercy Chaplet, Stations of the Cross, Morning & Evening Prayer, Angelus, Regina Caeli, Acts of Contrition, litanies, and much more.
- **Scripture Insights** — Reflections and commentary to deepen understanding of the daily readings.
- **Offline Bible Downloads** — Download additional translations for use anywhere, even without a connection.

## Daily Readings

From Advent through Ordinary Time, Catholic Daily delivers the complete lectionary readings:

- Colour-coded reading types — Gospel, Psalm, First & Second Reading
- Liturgical seasons, feasts, and memorials highlighted
- Navigate to any date with the built-in calendar
- Text-to-speech — listen to readings on the go

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Flutter (Dart)
- **Bible database:** RSV-CE (SQLite, bundled offline)
- **Lectionary data:** Standard + weekday lectionary CSVs, ordo resolver service
- **Platforms:** Android, iOS, Windows
- **Publisher:** [ElBiblio](https://elbiblio.com)
